<h4><strong>About Scouts</strong></h4>
<p>The CCA aims to nurture the holistic development of Cub Scouts within
an environment that fosters intellectual and physical growth, while also
presenting challenges for them to overcome. Through active participation,
Cub Scouts will learn valuable skills in self-management and peer support,
providing them with practical leadership experience.</p>

<h4><strong>CCA Highlight(s)</strong></h4>
<h4>ADIJI Chief Scout</h4>
<p>On March 2, 2024, our Cub Scouts from P4 to P6 joined 4000 other scouts
at Choa Chu Kang Stadium to welcome President Tharman Shamugaratnam as
Chief Scout. Our Cub Scouts hosted an engaging educational booth where
visitors learned how to fold various paper planes. The booth received a
positive reception, making it a memorable occasion for our Cub Scouts.</p>

<h4>Cub Scout Leadership Training Course</h4>
<p>Our Cub Scouts took part in the Cub Scout Leadership Training Course 2024
held in Evergreen Primary School. The course focuses on developing social-emotional
skills, leadership concepts, and activity planning through the Scouting
Method and Experiential Learning methods.</p>

<h4>Outdoor Cooking Competition</h4>
<p>Our Cub Scouts participated in the 2023 Outdoor Cooking Competition held
at Chongfu School. The primary aim of the event was to promote experiential
learning and teamwork through cooking. Our Cub Scouts had the chance to
showcase the skills they learned during our CCA sessions, resulting in
them winning 2 Gold and 1 Silver awards. Good job, Cub Scouts!</p>
