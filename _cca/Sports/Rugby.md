<h4><strong>About Rugby</strong></h4>
<p>MPS Rugby aims to equip students with the technical skills of how to play
rugby confidently and safely. Besides the physical, students learn values
such as teamwork, resilience and overcoming their fears and inhibitions.
Students have the opportunity to showcase their skills annually at the
National School Games. Additionally, the CCA aims to provide a pathway
for students to pursue the sport in secondary school through Direct School
Admission (DSA).</p>

<h4>Rugby National School Games (Senior &amp; Junior Division)</h4>
<p>The Rugby National School Games (NSG) is held annually to provide a platform
for our students to showcase their skills against other schools. The Senior
team comprises of our Primary 6s while our Junior team comprises of our
Primary 5s and 4s.</p>
