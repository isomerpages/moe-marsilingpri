<p>The Most Inspiring Tamil Teachers’ Award 2026 is jointly organised by
Tamil Murasu, Singapore Tamil Teachers’ Union and the Tamil Language Learning
and Promotion Committee. This award serves to recognise the stellar performance
of Tamil teachers in their role in educating and promoting Tamil Language
among their students.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Who qualifies for this award?</strong>
</p>
<p>This award is open to Tamil Teachers teaching in MOE schools and they
should be:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Teachers who nurture students’ interest in Tamil Language by using innovative
methods, including the effective use of educational technology to make
lessons more engaging and interactive; and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Dedicated and passionate teachers who demonstrate a keen interest in students’
well-being while developing 21st Century Competencies for them to be future-ready.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;<strong>Nomination Process:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>You can nominate Tamil teachers who meet the criteria mentioned above
via the nomination forms attached.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Teachers teaching in Primary, Secondary, Junior Colleges, Centralised
Institute and Specialised schools are eligible for nomination.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>School Leaders, students, parents, colleagues and former students are
encouraged to nominate the teachers via the <a href="https://go.gov.sg/mitt2026nominations" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">nomination form.</a>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>The closing date for nomination is Friday, 13 March 2026</strong>.</p>

</p>
