<p>The Arif Budiman Malay Language Teacher Award (AGAB) 2026 recognises the
contributions of Malay Language teachers in the teaching and learning of
the Malay Language. The award is open to all Malay Language teachers in
the local mainstream schools.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;Do nominate your deserving Malay Language teachers now by completing
the following&nbsp;<a href="https://go.gov.sg/agab-2026" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">nomination form.</a>
</p>
<p><strong>Nominations are open from 2nd February to 13th March 2026</strong>.</p>
