music.</p>

<h4><strong>Key Programmes</strong></h4>
<h4>Engaging With Songs, Our World Through Songs</h4>
<p>Primary 1 to Primary 4 students embark on a global musical journey in
this programme. They delve into a vibrant array of melodies and songs hailing
from diverse corners of the world. Beyond simply experiencing the tunes,
they immerse themselves in understanding, grasping, and mastering the musical
elements.</p>
<p>But there's more to it than just beats and rhythms! Our students are whisked
away into the rich tapestry of socio-historical narratives and cultural
landscapes associated with each song's country of origin. It's not just
about the music; it's a voyage through history and culture, all wrapped
up in the universal language of melody.</p>

<h4>From BUSK till dawn</h4>
<p>Get ready to watch our Primary 3 to Primary 6 students showcase their
talents in a supportive and non-competitive atmosphere at “from BUSK till
dawn"! Each term, for a span of two weeks, during recess, students have
the chance to take the stage to showcase their skills and passions.</p>
<p>The MPS community eagerly awaits these lively performances and dynamic
display of talent, which take place at the canteen. Standout acts discovered
during these sessions might even earn a spot at the Teachers' Day Concert,
showcasing their talent to the entire school!</p>

<h4>Music Masterclass</h4>
<p>Marsilians embark on a musical journey filled with growth, discovery,
and endless possibilities from 'In Curriculum Music Enrichment Programme’
- Movement through Music in Primary 1 and Voice Exploration in Primary
2. Exceptional students who are discovered through our 'In Curriculum Music
Enrichment Programme' will receive exclusive invitations to join Music
Masterclasses tailored to their respective levels, building upon the foundations
laid in their regular Music lessons. These sessions serve as an intermediate
exploration of musical forms, offering a deeper dive into their talent-identified
discipline.</p>
<p>But that's just the beginning! Participants will also have the opportunity
to further hone their talents by joining our Niche Performing Arts CCAs,
such as Choir, Chinese Dance, and Malay Dance. Representing the school
in the SYF Arts Presentation, they'll showcase their skills, enrich their
portfolios, and pave the way for future applications to Direct School Admission
(DSA) through CCA participation in Primary 6.</p>

<h4>Tech Tunes: Music Making using Technology</h4>
<p>Primary 5 and 6 students unlock their creative potential as they delve
into the exciting world of music improvisation, enhanced by technology.
Harnessing the affordances of GarageBand, students are given the autonomy
to express their musical creativity. Students get the opportunity to experiment
with rhythms and make informed decisions in their creative work that reflect
their understanding of musical concepts and elements acquired from Primary
1 to 4.</p>

<h4>Curriculum Enrichment Programme:&nbsp;</h4>
<h4>P1 Movement through Music and P2 Singing (Voice Exploration)</h4>
<p>Get ready for an immersive dance and vocal adventure. In Primary 1, students
explore the art of movement, using their bodies and surroundings to craft
dynamic dance moves inspired by diverse musical genres. Meanwhile, in Primary
2, it's all about finding your voice! Engaging in lively vocal exercises
and exciting song games, students learn to project their voices with precision
while mastering essential singing techniques and healthy breathing habits.
It's a journey of self-expression and skill-building that lays the foundation
for a lifelong love of music and performance!</p>
<p></p>

<h4>Jamming with Ukulele</h4>
<p>Dive into the captivating world of the ukulele! Students will learn a
diverse array of fundamental strumming patterns and techniques and conquer
the foundational chords of C, Am, F and G. Students build their confidence
during lessons, and some even performed at “from BUSK till dawn” to dazzle
audiences with their newfound skill. Let’s strum away!</p>

<h4>Hip Hop Hustle: Move &amp; Groove!</h4>
<p>Get ready to groove with our Primary 6 students as they dive into a series
of hip-hop dance lessons as part of their post-PSLE program. From mastering
cool moves to showcasing incredible resilience and teamwork, they come
together as a class to perfect and master their class's dance choreography.
The excitement doesn't stop there – during the P6 Appreciation Lunch, every
P6 class gets the spotlight to perform and cheer on their peers, creating
unforgettable moments of celebration and unity!</p>
