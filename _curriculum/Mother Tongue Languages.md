<h4><strong>Vision</strong></h4>
<p>Every Marsilian, an effective and confident Mother Tongue Language communicator
with deep appreciation for the Language and Culture.</p>
<p></p>
<h4><strong>Mission</strong></h4>
<p>The Mother Tongue Language Department aims:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To make language learning comes alive through immersive and interactive
lessons.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To instill the love of reading through the department Reading Programme.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To develop our Marsilians as proficient communicators of their MT language
in everyday life.</p>
</li>
</ul>

<h4><strong>Primary Mother Tongue Language Curriculum Framework</strong></h4>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/MTL/MTL_2.png">
</div>
<p>The 2024 Primary MTL Curriculum framework describe our aim for MTL Learners
to experience the joy of learning MTL that will help them to be confident
users through three curricular goals:</p>
<p>a) Language Competence- Students are equipped with foundational language
knowledge, skills and strategies to ensure effective communication.</p>
<p>b) Culture &amp; Values- Students are equipped with essential moral values
and are able to appreciate their own MTLs, culture and traditions; and</p>
<p>c)21CC-&nbsp; Students are equipped with the foundational socio-emotional
competencies and competencies such as civic, global and cross-cultural
literacy as well as critical, adaptive and inventive thinking.</p>
